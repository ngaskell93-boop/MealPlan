~750
kcal

Ingredients

  • 1 mediumcourgette, sliced into half-moons
  • 1 tbspolive oil
  • ½lemon, juiced
  • to tastesalt, black pepper, garlic powder
Method
  1. 1
    Preheat oven to 200°C (fan). Toss peppers and courgette in half the olive oil, season, and spread on a baking tray. Roast 20-22 minutes until tender and slightly charred.

  4. 4
    Rest chicken 3 minutes, then slice. Plate rice, top with sliced chicken and roasted veg. Squeeze lemon over everything before serving.

Intermittent Fasting Tips
Breaking the Fast
Start with a balanced lunch - protein + complex carbs + veg. Don't binge to compensate for the fast.
Hunger in the Morning
Black coffee, green tea, or sparkling water during the fast are your best tools. Hunger passes after 20-30 min.
Training on This Plan
Ideal to train around 11am (just before your window opens) or 12-1pm so you can refuel straight after.
Hydration
Aim for 2.5-3L of water across the full day. Electrolytes (a pinch of salt in water) help during longer fasts.
Protein Priority
Hit your 150g+ protein target every day. Spread it across all three meals - don't leave it all to dinner.
Progress Tracking
Weigh yourself weekly, same time, same conditions (morning, post-toilet). Expect 0.5-1 kg loss per week on track.
